高级口语 Class Rules and Schedule

Attendance

  • The purpose of this class is to practice oral english. As such, attendance is very important as it is very difficult to make up the experience outside of class.
    因为这门课的目的是让你练习口语，上课挺重要。没办法补课，所以每周必须来上课。
  • Miss 3 classes and you cannot take the final exam. If you have a serious extended situation, please contact me, so we can figure out a plan for you to pass.
    没上3次课的学生不能参加期末考试。
  • If you have a serious situation (e.g. a serious illness in the family, or a hospitalization), please message me before class to ask for leave. I may ask for some sort of proof.
    如果你有重要的事情，比如说家人生严重的病还是你住院，请课前联系我请假。我可能让你给我证明。
  • If a less serious issue comes up (e.g. a meeting for another class, non-serious illness, or catching a train), you can come to a different class in the same week to make up the absence.
    如果是不重要的事情，比如说专业开会，生不严重的病或者坐火车，请同周来上课。
  • Students who leave during the break period or use their cell phones excessively will only get half of the day’s attendance points.
    在休息时间逃课的学生，玩手机太多的学生只能得到半个学分。

Topics

  • I will post the topic on sunday before class.
    我在课前的周日要发话题。
  • Depending on the topic, I may post useful vocabulary.
    如果有用的话，我也要发单词。
  • The goal of vocabulary is to know how to use them, not just to memorize them. If you do not understand them, we can go over them in class.
    学单词的目的不在于让你记住，而是要会用。如果你不理解单词，请在课的时候提问。
  • You may prepare however you like. I make no guarantees about what the class activities are.
    你可以随便准备，我不要课前告诉你们课的活动。
  • I may change topics due to unforeseen circumstances.
    我可以随时改变话题。
